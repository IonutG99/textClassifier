The town of Târgovişte is undergoing modernization. The town hall decides to inventory all the buildings in the city in order to renovate their facades. In this sense, he analyzes the map of the city and finds that all the walls are placed only in the North South or East West direction. The walls visible to tourists are only those that can be reached from outside the city by moving in the two given directions, in any of the four directions (N, E, S, W). The city map is drawn on a grid made up of squares with side 1. Knowing the city map, determine the length of the visible walls to be painted.
