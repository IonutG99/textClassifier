Catalin is the most important general in his country's army, which has N members (soldiers and officers), numbered from 1 to N (Catalin is number 1). An officer is a soldier who has other soldiers under his command. According to the military hierarchy, Cătălin and all other officers initially receive 3 subordinates (soldiers with numbers 2, 3 and 4). Each of the three, in turn, receives 3 other subordinates, according to the following rule: the officer with number x receives subordinates 3 * x – 1, 3 * x, 3 * x + 1 (if they exist in the army).
