The plan of a square building consisting of n*n rooms is given, in the form of a matrix with n lines and n columns and elements 0 or 1. The rooms marked with 0 are free, the one marked with 1 are inaccessible and each room has a pair of coordinates , of the form I J, representing the line and column on which the room is located. You can move from one free room to another free room, provided that they are adjacent on a line or a column.
