Anarchy has been established in the evil Costel's empire. There are n cities in the empire, numbered from 1 to n, connected by m one-way roads, each road being controlled by a gang affiliated with one of the k bandit syndicates in the empire, numbered from 1 to k. To travel through the roads of the empire, any traveler must pay syndicate dues: paying the fee to a particular syndicate allows the traveler unlimited use of any road controlled by a gang affiliated with that syndicate. The same fee is paid for all unions.
