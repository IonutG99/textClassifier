Matei, who lives in town p, wants to go to town q to visit him. He has a map on which he is located n cities and m roads that he can pass through to reach his destination. The map also shows the time duration td for each road, which is the number of minutes it takes to travel the road and to for crossing cities (some cities are more crowded and some are not). Matei wants to reach his destination as quickly as possible, but if there are several minimum time routes, he will choose the one that passes through several cities. There will be no minimum time tracks with the same number of cities. Help Matei to find the required way.
