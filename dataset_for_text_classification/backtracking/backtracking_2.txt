A rectangular board consisting of n lines and m columns is given, defining n*m areas, some of them being free, others containing obstacles. In the area at position is, js there is a mouse that can move on the board passing from the current area to the adjacent area on the line or column. His goal is to reach a piece of cheese located in the area at position ib, jb, without leaving the board, without passing through areas containing obstacles, and without passing through the same area twice.
