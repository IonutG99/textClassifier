Most children are sociable and easily relate to those of their age, but there are also children who are more shy or less attracted to group activities. After studying the behavior of a sample of n children, it was established, for every two children, whether they relate or not. For the end-of-year celebration, it was proposed to make a skit and it is necessary to select as large a group of little ones as possible but without exceeding k, the maximum number of characters. The roles involve the interaction of each selected child with all the other small actors playing in the skit. Determine the largest number of children who can be involved in the celebration, knowing that each of them must relate to all the other children in the scene.
