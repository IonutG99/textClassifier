In the city of Imios there are n galleries with interesting locations to visit (museums, exhibitions, castles), rated from 1 to n. A gallery contains m locations, rated from 1 to m. Each location is assigned a number of points, unique in value. The city has many tourist information offices where tourists can buy a day card to visit the sites. The starting point is recorded on each card, denoted by 2 numbers: the number of the gallery and the number of the location, from which at most k locations in the city, different from the starting point, can be visited. The card has registered a number of points, which will be used in the locations visited.
